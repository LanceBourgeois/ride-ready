Just in case

🆘 If something goes wrong

🛑Feels wobbly or too fast? Ease off the throttle, brake smooth, coast down. Don't panic-grab.
⚡Something feels off (brakes, power, noise)? Stop riding. Tell Dad. Don't "ride it out."
🔋Charging: right charger only, never overnight unattended, never a hot/damaged battery.
🚑Crash: get out of the way, then check yourself. Head hit? Tell an adult even if you feel fine.
