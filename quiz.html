🛑 Why 5 km/h is a big deal

Rough distance to stop on a scooter (react + brake). Notice 45 km/h isn't 3× of 15 — it's about 5×. Stopping distance grows with speed squared.

⚖️The law: 16+ and 25 km/h on public roads — your scooter & age = private property for now.
🛑You can't out-brake a car. A little scooter stops worse than you'd think — never dart in front of traffic.
⚡Speed² rules everything. A bit more speed = a lot more stopping distance and a lot more crash energy.
👀Most crashes = not being seen at intersections/driveways, plus solo falls. Assume you're invisible.
⛑️Your head is the risk. Helmet, every ride. No exceptions.
